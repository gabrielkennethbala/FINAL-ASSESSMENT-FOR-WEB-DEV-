Sonder, the wandering company

We are the drifters belonging from worlds forgotten, collecting artifacts of forgotten beauty. The hunt for the extraordinary piques the curiosity of our company, and as such, we appear to appease the hunger of customers who seek either the "thrill" of the product or those that hunger for the "power" the products hold. From necklaces forged from frozen suns or rings that hum with intense holy or evil power, each piece in our collection carries a story, a fragment of the realms and universes far beyond the known limit of the visible universe We do not choose our customers, fate does. When an item calls to you, you answer, for it calls ONLY the one meant to hold it
